About Us

The Lagos State University Campus Radio 95.7 FM, a.k.a. Lively and Solidly Unique was established in 2016. It is a developmental radio and its mission is to positively transform the Students, Staff and the University Community.

The radio station is location at the Lagos State University Main Campus, Ojo, Lagos State Nigeria.
